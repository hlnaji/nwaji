The Nwaji

The Nwaji tribe is one of the Arab tribes residing in the Arabian Peninsula. It is considered a part of the Bedouin Arab tribes that traditionally engage in nomadic lifestyles and herding in the desert. There are several branches of the Nwaji tribe in various Arab countries such as Saudi Arabia, Yemen, Jordan, Iraq, Syria, Kuwait, Egypt, Mali and Morocco. Historically, the Nwaji tribe has played a significant role in Religion, trade, protection, and politics in the region.
